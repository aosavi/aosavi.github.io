## Modern Measurement

Honours Bachelor course on state-of-the-art research methods in Psychology. Several guest lecturers contribute to the course and topics include machine learning, networks, A/B testing, and N=1 timeseries&#151;in collaboration with <a href="https://www.uva.nl/over-de-uva/organisatie/medewerkers/content/l/i/m.a.vanderlinden/m.a.vanderlinden.html">Max van der Linden</a>.
